How long does it take to build an AI system?

Most projects take 2–6 weeks depending on complexity. A basic AI chatbot with RAG can be deployed in 1–2 weeks. A full Voice AI Calling Agent with CRM integration typically takes 3–4 weeks of active engineering.

Do you offer on-premise deployment?

Yes. All our AI systems can be deployed on your own infrastructure — AWS, GCP, Azure, or your own servers. For sensitive enterprise data, we recommend on-premise deployment with no data leaving your environment.

What's the cost of building a Voice AI Calling Agent?

Pricing depends on call volume, integration complexity, and custom features. Contact us for a detailed quote — we offer project-based pricing and monthly retainer options for ongoing support.

Do you provide post-deployment support?

Yes — all projects include 30 days of free support post-launch. Long-term maintenance contracts with SLA guarantees are available for production systems.
