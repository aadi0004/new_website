FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of AI systems does QuantumAI build?

We design and ship 5 core AI systems: RAG-powered Knowledge Bases (connect your internal docs to an AI that answers employee and customer queries instantly), AI Agents & Automation (end-to-end workflows using LangChain + tool-calling agents), Voice AI Calling Agents (autonomous outbound calling, lead qualification, and appointment booking), ContentForge AI (upload media → AI writes titles, captions, and hashtags and posts to YouTube, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook simultaneously), and Custom ML Engineering (predictive models, recommendation engines, and analytics pipelines tailored to your data).

How long does it take to deploy an AI solution?

Timelines depend on the project tier: Pilot (Starter) — a single focused AI agent (e.g., internal FAQ bot, lead capture agent) goes live in 2–4 weeks. Growth — a multi-agent system with integrations (CRM, WhatsApp, calendar) takes 4–8 weeks. Enterprise — full-scale transformation with custom ML models, on-premise deployment, and team training is scoped individually. We always begin with a 30-minute free strategy call to give you an accurate quote.

What technologies and LLMs do you work with?

We're model-agnostic. Our preferred stack: LLMs — OpenAI GPT-4o, Google Gemini 1.5 Pro, Anthropic Claude 3, and open-source models (Llama 3, Mistral) for cost-efficient deployments. RAG & Memory — Pinecone, ChromaDB, FAISS, MongoDB Atlas Vector Search. Orchestration — LangChain, LangGraph, CrewAI. Backend — FastAPI, Node.js/Express. Frontend — React, Next.js. Infra — AWS, GCP, Azure, Docker, Vercel. We select the right stack based on your performance, cost, and data-privacy requirements.

How does the Voice AI Calling Agent actually work?

Our Voice AI Agent operates in 4 phases: 1. Outbound Dial — the agent initiates calls from your number list using Twilio/VAPI. 2. Natural Conversation — powered by real-time STT (speech-to-text) + GPT-4o, it speaks naturally, handles objections, and qualifies leads using your custom script. 3. Appointment Booking — confirmed slots are written directly to Google Calendar / Calendly and a confirmation SMS is sent to the prospect. 4. Follow-Up — personalized follow-up emails are triggered automatically post-call. Everything runs 24/7 without human intervention. Average handling time: under 3 minutes per call.

What is ContentForge AI and how does it post to multiple platforms?

ContentForge AI is our multimodal content engine. You simply upload a photo or video and provide a short hint about the topic. The AI then: analyzes the media with vision models, generates an optimized title, description, hashtags, and alt-text for each platform's algorithm, and then cross-posts to YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, and LinkedIn simultaneously via their official APIs. It drastically reduces content production time from hours to minutes. It's available as a standalone SaaS product or as an add-on to any custom AI deployment.
